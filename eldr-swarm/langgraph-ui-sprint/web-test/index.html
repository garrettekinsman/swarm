The Three Pillars of Security

Built on cryptographic principles to ensure your communication remains private, secure, and yours alone.

Sealed with NIP-17

True end-to-end encryption. Your messages are sealed before they leave your device and only unlocked by your recipient's key. Not even relay operators can read them.

No Servers to Seize

Nostr is a decentralized relay network. There is no central database to hack, no corporation to subpoena. Your data exists only where you allow it.

Self-Sovereign Identity

You are not a phone number. You are a cryptographic key pair. Create, backup, and restore your identity without asking permission from anyone.

How It Works

Simple steps to cryptographic sovereignty. No technical background required.

01

Generate Your Keys

EldrChat creates a cryptographic identity (npub) stored only on your device. No email required. Your private keys never leave your device.

02

Connect to Relays

Choose which Nostr relays to join—public ones for discovery, private ones for stealth. You're in complete control over where your data travels.

03

Speak Freely

Message anyone on the Nostr network. Your words travel encrypted, hopping between relays until they reach their destination—securely and privately.
